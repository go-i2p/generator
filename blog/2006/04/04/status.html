* 1) Net status and 0.6.1.14

The 0.6.1.13 release is still a bit bumpy out there, though we've
had some substantial fixes and improvements in subsequent builds.

* 2) Syndie plotting

There've been a bunch of discussions regarding syndie revamps,
including GUIs, browser plugins, full text search, a more complete
syndication scheduler, an archive manager, multipage posts, support
for more formatting options, and a whole bunch more.  I expect we'll
be getting way on a substantial set of mods pretty soon, so if you
want to get involved, or get your ideas implemented, speak up :)

* 3) Local jbigi optimizations

There have been reports of even further jbigi optimization for amd64
users, and while jbigi probably isn't near the choke point for those
